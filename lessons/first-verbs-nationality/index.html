First verbs; nationality; languages; “Where are you from?”

Откуда Вы? Вы говорите по-русски?

In this lesson you will learn how to say what country you come from, what your nationality is and what language you speak. Key phrases — Я из Англии, я англичанин, я говорю по-английски. Also you will find an introduction to Russian verbs.
