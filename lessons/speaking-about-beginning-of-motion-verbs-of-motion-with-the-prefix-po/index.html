Speaking about the beginning of Motion. Verbs of Motion with the prefix "по-"

Летом мы поедем на дачу

In this lesson you will begin studying Verbs of Motion with prefixes. You will take a look at the prefix по-, its meanings and learn how to use it.

For example:

Завтра он пойдёт к врачу.

Через год она поедет в США.
