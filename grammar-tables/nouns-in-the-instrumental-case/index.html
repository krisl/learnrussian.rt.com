Instrumental Case answers questions Кем? Чем? and, it is easy to guess, is used to express the concerpt of an instrument.

  • Nom. Sing.
  • Verb
  • Instr. Sing.

Константин пишет карандашом.

Konstantin writes with a pensil.

  • Nom. Sing.
  • Verb
  • Instr. Sing.

Егор Петрович ест салат вилкой.

Egor Petrovich eats his salad with a fork.

Instrumental is often used with preposition с to indicate that subject does anything together with an object.

  • Nom. Sing.
  • Verb
  • Instr. Sing.

Наталия ходила в театр с братом.

Natalia went to the theatre with her brother.

  • Nom. Sing.
  • Verb
  • Instr. Sing.

Александр пьет кофе с молоком.

Alexander drinks coffee with milk.

Remember

Masculine words that end with a vowel (папа, дедушка, etc) will change their endings as their feminine counterparts do
(с папой, с дедушкой).

  • дочь → с дочерью
  • мать → с матерью

There are no animate neuter gender nouns.
