Lesson 1

Professions can be either masculine (артист – artist, журналист – journalist) or feminine (артистка, журналистка). There are also some professions that can be both—masculine and feminine, depending on gender of a person you are speaking about (менеджер – manager, дизайнер – designer). Here is the list of some commonly used professions.
