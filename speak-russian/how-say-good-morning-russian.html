How to Say Good Morning in Russian

So you want to know how to say good morning in Russian. The first way can be translated directly to and from English. Good morning in Russian is “dobroe utro” - доброе утро (dough-bro-ye oo-tro). Another way to say good morning in Russian is “S dobrym utrom” - С добрым утром (s dough-breem oo-trum). These are totally interchangeable so feel free to choose whatever you feel like using when wanting to say good morning in Russian. Another way you can say good morning in Russian is by using just good which is “dobroe” - доброе (dough-bro-ye). You can’t get away with simply saying “morning” like you can in English, though. Knowing how to say good morning in Russian will help you to start your day off right in Russia. It’s always pleasant to get this greeting as your day starts - whether it be at home or in the office place. But people usually don’t use it with strangers passing by on the street.
