How to Write “I Love You” in Russian

Maybe you are involved in a long-distance relationship. Maybe your significant other knows Russian and you want to impress him or her. Or maybe you just want to show off your language-writing skills. Whatever the case, we will show you how to write “I love you” in Russian. I love you in Russian is “Ya lyublyu tebya” (ya lu-BLUE teeb-YA). Я люблю тебя. Another way of how to write “I love you” in Russian is in the plural/formal. Here you would say “Ya lyublui vas” (ya lu-BLUE vas). Я люблю вас. Use it when you want to write your love for many people or for a special one you still haven’t started speaking informally with yet. How to write “I love you” in Russian is something RT Learn Russian can show you how to do with your keyboard, but you might just want to copy and paste what we have posted. It’s much quicker and easier that way. Plus, whoever you are trying to impress will just as well think you know how to write “I love you” in Russian.
