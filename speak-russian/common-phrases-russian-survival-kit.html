Common Phrases in Russian — Survival Kit

If your Russian is not fluent enough yet, it is no reason to let the conversation freeze. We have compiled a list of common phrases in Russian to help you out in everyday language situations. ‘Privet’ for ‘hi’ and ‘kak dela?’ for ‘how are you?’ are good conversation-openers and perhaps the most common phrases in Russian one uses hundreds of times daily. ‘Vsyo khorosho’ which translates as ‘everything is fine’ is a way to answer the question ‘kak dela?’. ‘Chem zanimaeshsya?’, also one of the most common phrases in Russian, stands for ‘what are you doing?’ and is a natural way to show a Russian your sincere interest in continuing the conversation.
