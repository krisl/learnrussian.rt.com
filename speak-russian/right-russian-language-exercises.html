Finding the Right Russian Language Exercises

Although Russian is often classified amongst the hardest languages to learn, there is no reason why it cannot be a fun task. One just has to find the proper tools to achieve language proficiency. Finding the right Russian language exercises has been made easy with RT. The course provides you with a wide range of language activities covering all major topics from pronunciation to vocabulary to grammar for learners of all levels. The Russian language exercises on this website feature a variety of assignments to help you develop and polish listening, reading, speaking, and writing skills. All of them have a key so the course is suitable for self-study. RT is offering you a program of study designed as a consolidated course with every lesson containing Russian language exercises which are based on what you have learned to that point, all the while preparing you for new material to come. Russian language exercises help you put theory into practice and see how natural Russian works in real-life situations.
