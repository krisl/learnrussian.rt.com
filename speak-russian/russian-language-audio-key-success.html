Russian Language Audio—Key to Success

Any language, unless it is dead, is spoken, which in most cases involves components of listening and understanding. Part of your success in mastering a foreign language will come from listening to it ‘in the wild’, as it is spoken in real-life situations. Access to Russian language audio on this website is key to polishing your language skills. The Russian language audio section features a wide array of professional and real native voices, giving you the opportunity to hear what natural Russian sounds like. The Russian language audio which the course provides covers all major topics from the Russian alphabet and basic survival phrases to intonation patterns and complex conversation. The Russian language audio section is built to help learners of all levels improve their listening, comprehension, and conversation skills through step-by-step immersion in natural Russian speech. Russian language audio comes in a variety of exercises and activities alongside grammar and vocabulary topics discussed in the course.
