Learn Russian on YouTube

We live in a digital society and part of being a responsible citizen of this community is to use resources out there to improve ourselves and our knowledge. Online videos are abundant these days, so why not learn Russian on YouTube? RT’s Learn Russian project is filled with video resources designed to help you master the language. From word lessons to street polls, we have created and compiled an array of material and have posted it online for you to learn Russian on YouTube. RT Learn Russian’s YouTube channel is a great multimedia tool for your language-learning needs. It is designed to be a component of a much more comprehensive course which includes grammar, audio, visual aids, and more! But if you want to learn Russian on YouTube, you will definitely want to check out our channel - where you will find fun and interactive material and links to even more! Feel free to favorite our clips, subscribe to the channel, and comments are always more than welcome!
