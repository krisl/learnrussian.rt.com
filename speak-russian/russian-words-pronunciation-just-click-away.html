Russian Words and Pronunciation—Just a Click Away!

So you have decided to learn Russian. This is a great idea and we will be here for you every step of the way. RT Russian has everything you need. If you are new to the language, chances are what you are looking for are key Russian words and pronunciation. You are in luck, because, of course, this is part of the RT Russian program! We have compiled vocabulary lists and even provide a “Word of the Day” for all your trivial needs. Knowing the most important Russian words and pronunciation will enable you to speak Russian and do so efficiently—that is to say, so that people will understand you! Also included in RT Russian are pronunciation guides and a collection of audio and video files with real Russians speaking their language that you can learn from. Russian words and pronunciation will be things that you don’t even think about the further you progress in the course and more second-nature the language becomes to you. With the materials provided in this course, you’ll be listening, understanding, and speaking in no time!
