Everything You Need to Learn to Speak Russian Is at Your Fingertips!

RT Russian is filled with loads of resources designed for you to learn to speak Russian. For years, there have been many Russian specialists working in academia and other research organizations that have developed a strong reading and writing knowledge of Russian. But if you want to learn to speak Russian the tools you need are available right here in this course. Since we are based in Moscow, we’ve got a constant and abundant supply of real Russian speakers and voices to help you learn to speak Russian. Whether you want to listen to audio dialogues, explanatory podcasts, or even watch Russians answer questions in unscripted speech, you’ll find everything you are looking for - and more - on our site. Once you decide you want to learn to speak Russian, you will likely want to find other people who speak Russian and practice with them. We have a database of language schools and courses around the world and also an online tutor finder. The best way to learn to speak Russian is to simply speak! Nobody is perfect and it is only through our mistakes that we can understand how to improve. So approach the whole learn to speak Russian task as one that is manageable, fun, and rewarding - because it truly can be and is with us!
